The Ranch

A first-class golf venue, built on family land

Christie's Golf Ranch is a one-of-a-kind driving range and nine-hole par-3 course nestled in the heart of horse country in Pilot Point. It began as the life-long dream of golf enthusiast Jeff Christie, who bought the land in 2008 and built the venue in honor of his late father, Jim Christie.

Today it's a full-service, all-grass practice facility — fifty-five stations, a pro shop, professional instruction, memberships, and a junior camp — where individuals, groups, and families build real skills on well-kept grounds.

"There are not many people living out their dream. I am doing that." — Jeff Christie
